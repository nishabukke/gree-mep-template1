Then there is pressure on the quality of water too, since polluted water is now a reality in our cities, towns and villages. So , from water supply to drainage and sewage treatment each stage is important and needs specialist services. We offer a variety of plumbing services. Electrical Engineering

Lighting—indoor and outdoor—accounts for a significant proportion of electricity demands in a project. GREE MEP engineers can help optimize your costs through system designs and recommendation of equipment and automation techniques that can help you reduce your building’s lighting consumption in a major way.
